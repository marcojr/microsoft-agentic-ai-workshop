# Delivery Delay Policy

## Standard
Orders marked as delayed must be reviewed against the carrier status, estimated delivery date, and customer promise date.

## Escalation
- Delay under 24 hours: monitor and inform the customer.
- Delay above 24 hours: open a support follow-up.
- Delay with financial impact or repeated failure: raise approval workflow.

## Customer Communication
Messages must state the factual delay reason and the latest delivery expectation.
