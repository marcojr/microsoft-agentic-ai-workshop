# Customer Compensation Policy

## Scope
Applies to customer-facing compensation decisions linked to delivery issues, refunds, and service failures.

## Approval Rules
- Below GBP 50: team lead can approve.
- GBP 50 to GBP 500: service manager approval required.
- Above GBP 500: director approval required.
- Any external communication about compensation must be approved before being sent.

## Eligibility
Customers with repeated SLA failures or significant delivery disruption can receive credit or refund support.

## Process
1. Create an approval request.
2. Link the order, shipment, and refund records.
3. Wait for approval before confirming compensation to the customer.
